About 24HourWire

News from multiple perspectives

What is 24HourWire?

24HourWire aggregates news headlines from sources across the political spectrum, in multiple languages. We show you the same stories from different outlets so you can see how coverage varies based on source bias.

We only keep stories from the last 24 hours to keep things fresh and relevant.

Languages

24HourWire supports multiple languages. Use the language links in the header to switch between them. Each language has its own set of sources from that language's media landscape.

Understanding the Links

"Read full story ↗" - Takes you directly to the original article on the publisher's website.

"Find coverage ↗" - Searches Bing News for related coverage of the same story. This helps you find how other outlets are reporting the same event. Click this to compare coverage across different sources.

"Bias rating" - Links to the Media Bias/Fact Check page for that source, showing their detailed bias analysis.

The "Most Covered" Tab

This tab shows stories that appear across 2 or more of your selected sources. The number shown (e.g., "3 sources") indicates how many outlets are covering that particular story.

We group stories by comparing their headlines. Common words (like "the", "a", "in") are filtered out so matching is based on meaningful keywords. If 60% or more of the remaining words overlap, the stories are grouped together.

Source Categories

Use the dropdown to filter by bias category:

  • Center sources (default) - Shows only wire services and neutral outlets
  • All sources - Shows everything
  • Center/Left-Center/Left/Right only - Filter to specific bias categories
  • Clear all - Hides all sources (useful if you want to pick individual ones)

Click the source count next to the dropdown to see exactly which sources are currently selected.

How We Stay Legal

We respect copyright and fair use:

  • We show only headlines and brief summaries (not full articles)
  • We link directly to the original publisher
  • We attribute each story to its source
  • We use non-personalized ads (no tracking cookies)

Auto-Updates

News is fetched automatically every 15 minutes from all sources. You don't need to refresh - just visit the site to see the latest headlines.
