Fair Use & Copyright Policy

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

Summary: 24HourWire is a news aggregation service that displays headlines and brief summaries from publicly available news sources. We believe our use constitutes fair use under U.S. copyright law (17 U.S.C. § 107). All content is attributed to its original source, and we provide direct links to the full articles. Stories are displayed for a maximum of 24 hours and are automatically deleted thereafter.

1. Our Aggregation Model

24HourWire operates as a news discovery and comparison platform. We:

  • Display only headlines and brief neutral summaries (typically 1-3 sentences)
  • Never display full articles, images, videos, or substantial portions of original content
  • Always attribute content to its original source with prominent outbound links
  • Automatically delete all stories after 24 hours
  • Do not require user accounts or subscriptions to access content

2. Fair Use Analysis (17 U.S.C. § 107)

Our use of news content is intended to qualify as fair use under U.S. copyright law based on the following four factors:

Purpose and Character of Use

Our use is transformative. We do not merely republish news; we aggregate, organize, and contextualize headlines from multiple sources to allow users to compare coverage across the political spectrum. This comparative function provides new meaning and utility not present in the original works.

Nature of the Copyrighted Work

News articles contain factual information, which receives less copyright protection than creative works. Headlines themselves are generally not copyrightable as they represent facts and ideas.

Amount and Substantiality

We use only the minimal amount necessary: headlines and brief summaries that do not reveal the heart of the original article. Our summaries are algorithmically generated or excerpted to avoid reproducing the creative expression of the original authors.

Effect on Market

Our service is designed to drive traffic to original publishers by providing prominent direct links. We do not compete with publishers' subscription models or advertising revenue. Users must visit the original source to read full articles.

3. Attribution & Source Links

Every story displayed on 24HourWire includes:

  • Clear attribution to the original publisher
  • Direct hyperlink to the original article
  • Publication timestamp
  • Link to the publisher's Media Bias/Fact Check rating

We encourage all users to click through to the original sources to support quality journalism.

4. Content Removal & Takedown Requests

If you are a copyright holder and believe your work has been used in a manner that exceeds fair use, we will promptly address your concerns.

To Submit a Takedown Request:

Send an email to admin@24hourwire.news with:

  1. Your name and contact information
  2. Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to be infringed
  3. URL(s) of the content on 24HourWire you want removed
  4. A statement that you have a good faith belief the use is not authorized
  5. A statement that the information is accurate, under penalty of perjury
  6. Your electronic or physical signature

We will respond to all takedown requests within 48 hours and remove disputed content promptly upon verification.
